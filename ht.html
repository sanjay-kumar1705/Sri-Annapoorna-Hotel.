About Sri Annapoorna

Established in 1970, Hotel Sri Annapoorna has been serving delicious vegetarian cuisine for more than 55 years. Our commitment to fresh, healthy, and tasty food has made us a favorite in Salem. We cherish the legacy of providing authentic South Indian and Chinese dishes in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
